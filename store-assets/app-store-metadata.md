### Description
Mess&Anger is a secure, private, peer-to-peer messaging app that puts you back in control of your conversations. No servers, no surveillance, no compromises.

**Why Mess&Anger?**

🔒 **End-to-End Encrypted** — Every message is encrypted on your device and decrypted only on the recipient's device. We never have access to your conversations.

📡 **True Peer-to-Peer** — Messages go directly between devices. No central servers storing your data, metadata, or message history.

📱 **No Phone Number Required** — Sign up with a username only. No SIM, no carrier, no personal identifiers. Your identity is your keypair, not your phone number.

🔐 **Post-Quantum Cryptography** — Built with future-proof cryptography that resists attacks from quantum computers. Your secrets stay secret.

🌐 **Open Source** — The entire codebase is publicly auditable. No black boxes, no hidden backdoors, no trust required.

🕶️ **Anonymous by Design** — No email, no phone, no ID verification. Create an identity in seconds and start messaging instantly.

⚡ **Progressive Web App** — Works as a native app via PWA. No app store required to use, but available here for convenience.

**Features:**
- One-to-one and group messaging
- Disappearing messages
- Message reactions
- File and image sharing (E2EE)
- Contact discovery via public key exchange
- Offline message queuing with delivery when peer comes online
- Cross-platform — works on any device with a modern browser
